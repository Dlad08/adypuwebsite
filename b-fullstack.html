B.Tech Computer Engineering – Full Stack Developement

One of the most talked-about courses, Full Stack Development, is the manner of creating, building, and designing a product through all the engineering stages and layers by a single person. The engineer here who accomplishes this arduous task of including all efficiency layers into one is known as the jack-of-all-trades, or clearly the Full Stack Developer.

about course


The B.tech in computer science with the specialization in full stack development is exclusively designed as per the latest industry trends. This course adds to devise a complete packaged professional who can handle the development of a product from the start to the finish point single-handedly. The content is created by highly skilled experts and delivered by well renowned industry experts. The program gives you an extra edge than a basic B.tech degree course.


The Curriculum


We have designed Best-in-class content by leading faculty and industry leaders in the form of videos, cases and projects, assignments and live sessions. The nine specialization electives are spread across the whole four years of B.tech. Starting from fundamentals of programming which includes data structures and algorithms. Software design is order to understand the core design of software development. As for the frontend,we have covered html,css to advanced javascript along with the latest framework-ReactJs. The backend part covers the java based web developments covering spring framework along with API’s. Not of this,the program offers a thorough understanding of Devops and project deployment.
