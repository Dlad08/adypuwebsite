about me

Dr. Ipsita has completed her B-Tech from a state government college, Odisha in Mechanical Engineering, and has a Ph.D. in “Nanoporous material for energy storage devices” at the Department of Materials Engineering, NIT-Rourkela. She holds the award of DAAD scholarship from Forschungszentrum Jülich, Germany under the IEK-1 team. Her current area of research includes material engineering for energy storage devices, drug delivery, robotics, and automation. She has a keen interdisciplinary approach to her research work and aspires to continue to collaborate in different fields.
